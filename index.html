BACKDROP

I am a Washington-DC based photographer who primarily shoots on 35mm film. I enjoy exploring new places and capturing the unnoticed or overlooked moments in everyday life, showcasing the majestey hidden around us. When not taking photos, I am a full-time investigative analyst within the government where I produce tactical reporting based on data analysis, geospacial analysis, and financial analysis. Photograhy, for me, serves as a necessary interlude between my public life and my private life, it allows me to reset my mind, adapt my thinking, and take on new perspectives which may allow me to see a situation differently than I previously had.
